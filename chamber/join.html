Membership levels

Non-Profit: No membership fee required

  • Networking Opportunities: Access to networking events with local businesses and other non-profits.
  • Visibility: Listing in the chamber's directory as a non-profit member.
  • Educational Resources: Access to workshops and seminars on topics relevant to non-profits.
  • Advocacy Support: Opportunities to participate in advocacy efforts related to non-profit interests.

Bronze Membership:

  • Basic Benefits Package: Access to essential resources and services.
  • Networking Events: Invitations to regular networking events and mixers.
  • Member-to-Member Discounts: Access to exclusive discounts offered by other chamber members.
  • Newsletter Subscription: Subscription to the chamber's newsletter with updates on local business news and events.
  • Educational Workshops: Participation in introductory workshops on business topics like marketing, finance, and management.

Silver Membership:

  • Enhanced Benefits Package: Includes all Bronze benefits plus additional perks.
  • Priority Networking: Priority invitations to networking events and access to exclusive networking opportunities.
  • Featured Listing: Enhanced listing in the chamber's directory with a detailed profile and logo.
  • Business Referrals: Referrals to Silver and Gold members through the chamberâ€™s business referrals program.
  • Advanced Workshops: Access to advanced workshops and seminars tailored to business growth strategies.
  • Social Media Promotion: Promotion of business milestones and events on the chamber's social media channels.

Gold Membership:

  • Premium Benefits Package: Includes all Silver benefits plus exclusive perks.
  • VIP Events: Invitations to VIP events, including exclusive receptions and business leader roundtables.
  • Personalized Business Assistance: Dedicated account manager for personalized support and assistance with chamber services.
  • Sponsorship Opportunities: Priority sponsorship opportunities at chamber events and programs.
  • Board and Committee Involvement: Eligibility for leadership roles on chamber committees or the board of directors.
  • Strategic Partnership: Annual strategic consultation with chamber leadership to discuss business needs and opportunities for collaboration.
