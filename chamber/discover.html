Tampico facts

- In 2020, the population in Tampico was 297,562 inhabitants (47.6% men and 52.4% women).

- During the period of Mexico's first oil boom in the early 20th century, the city was the "chief oil-exporting port of the Americas" and the second-busiest in the world,

Events

1st golf tournament


The golf tournament at the Tampico Country Club concluded with great success on Friday, August 20. Around 120 players, lovers and fans of this sport came to the facilities of this club to play and win incredible prizes.
At the end of the tournament, the best players were awarded, who won incredible prizes such as cash and objects of use for golfers: balls, bag of clubs, coolers, among other things. The grand prize was a motorcycle that was randomly drawn to a player for his effort and dedication in this sporting event.

Tampico's Demographics

International sales of Tampico in 2023 were US$54.2M, 38.4% more than the previous year. The products with the highest level of international sales in 2023 were Live Animals of the Bovine Species (US$16.3M), Wrap Sets Knitted or Sport (Training Sports), Monos (Coveralls) and Sets Ski and Swimwear; other Apparel (US$5.14M), and Constructions and Parts Thereof (Bridges and Parts Thereof, Towers, Doors and Windows and their Frames) of Iron or Steel, Excluding Prefabricated Buildings (US$4.39M).
